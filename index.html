Welcome to my website!

I am an international research professional with over a decade of international research expertise, a strong track record in planning and executing social research initiatives.

My primary focus within the realm of development advisory revolves around key areas such as financial inclusion, digital financial services, gender equality, women's economic empowerment, education, and poverty assessment.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of collaborating with esteemed institutions and organizations, including the United Nations Capital Development Fund (UNCDF), the Department for International Development (DFID) - U.K., The World Bank, GIZ, SDC, as well as influential non-governmental organizations like the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation and the Walmart Foundation, among others.

I excel in crafting research designs, developing research tools, designing evaluations, conducting data analysis, crafting comprehensive reports, shaping proposals, and creating knowledge products. With experience spanning ten diverse countries, I've concentrated much of my development advisory work in the South Asian region, including India, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Myanmar, as well as the Pacific Island Countries.

Rahul Sinha is an international development professional with over 11 years of experience in research and evaluation in the domain of digital and financial inclusion, agriculture, WASH and Women's Economic Empowerment. He has a wide range of experience in evaluating programs, using evidence to inform policy decision-making, and conveying findings to diverse audiences. His main area of expertise lies in finalizing research design, research instruments, evaluation design, data analysis, report writing, and developing knowledge products.

Rahul is an Information Science and Engineering graduate and completed his post-graduation in Rural Management from Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar.
